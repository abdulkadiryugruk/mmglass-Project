Our History

M&M Glass was founded in 1984 by Robert Mitchell (Mr. Bob) as a company that installed affordable, high-quality shower doors and, in most cases, installed them the very next-day.. After joining forces with his son-in-law, Chris Van Pelt, the company’s product line and services expanded quickly to include an ever larger range of glass and
mirror products.

M&M has developed and nurtured strong relationships with our vendors and manufacturers over the years which has allowed us to never waiver from providing our customers with the best possible products at the best price, installed in their homes within days of approval.

Even though we’ve grown a lot since Mr. Bob’s one-man operation, we continue to work as a family business (because we are one). We value our employees and it shows. A number of our employees have been with us for 10, 20, and even 30 years (and counting)!

M&M is the go-to choice when you need reliable, quality glass products delivered and installed on time and on budget.

We are proud to serve the majority of South Louisiana (New Orleans, Northshore, Covington, Mandeville, Houma, Ascension Parish, St. Francisville, Zachary, East and West Baton Rouge, Port Allen, Lafayette, Pride) and even customers’ vacation homes in Gulf-Shores and Pensacola.
