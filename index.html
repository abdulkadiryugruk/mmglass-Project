4 Reasons to Choose Us

  • Quality Products and Services

    We take pride in our reputation throughout South Louisiana. For over three generations we have
    provided our customers with the best quality glass products, at the best possible price. We are able to
    offer this because of the strong relationships we have built and nurtured over the years with our
    vendors. Every product installed by our team is backed by a 365-day, 100% satisfaction guarantee.

  • Free Consultation and Estimate

    Ready to begin planning your design vision? We’re ready to help! Our dedicated Sales & Design Team offers free consultations on every potential job. Whether you are looking to meet privately or with your team (designer or builder), we are happy to accommodate you. Our team is armed with frame and glass samples, our project portfolio, product catalogs, and decades of know-how every single time.

  • Our Award Winning Team Handles Your Project From Start to Finish

    From our very first interaction, our professional and experienced team handles every step of your project. We handle the measurements, design consultations, custom cut, polish, beveling, framing, and manufacturing of your glass and mirror, and our award winning installation team finishes it all off. Whether you prefer to stop by our shop or meet with us at your place, our team is there to help every step of the way.

  • We’re Experts in the Home Building Industry

    Our company is proud to be a member of the Northshore Home Builders Association, Home Builders Association of Greater Baton Rouge, Acadiana Home Builders, North Shore Home Builders, Home Builders of Greater New Orleans, and the Louisiana Home Builders Association. We are passionate about home building, renovation, and design, and our team is always abreast of the latest trends, techniques and products. When you choose M&M, you can trust that you’ve chosen the best.
